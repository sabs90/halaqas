## Key Outreach Principles

1. **Be genuine, not salesy.** This is a community service, not a product. People can tell the difference. Share it because it's useful, not because you need downloads.

2. **Let WhatsApp do the work.** Every shared event link is free marketing. The share button is your most important growth feature.

3. **Make mosques look good.** Frame every outreach as "we're helping more people attend your events." Never position Halaqas as competing with mosques' own channels.

4. **Respect the community's time.** Keep messages short. Don't spam. Don't over-follow-up. If a mosque or community member isn't interested, move on.

5. **Be responsive.** If someone reports a bug or suggests an improvement, act fast. Early adopters who feel heard become advocates.

6. **Don't over-promise.** Launch with what works. It's better to have 20 well-maintained mosques than 200 stale listings. Quality builds trust.
